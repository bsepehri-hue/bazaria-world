Bazaria Dual-Rail Architecture

We have successfully decoupled your platform into two distinct "Rails." They operate independently but report to the same Dashboard.

Rail 1: The Digital Marketplace (The Smart Contract)

Purpose: Peer-to-peer USDC sales and NFT pass-through transfers.

Technology: BazariaNFTMarket.sol (Blockchain).

Logic: The smart contract handles the escrow (the USDC swap for the NFT).

Fee Capture: The 3% platform fee is deducted in USDC on-chain and sent to your treasuryWallet.

Agent Tracking: The ItemSold event is broadcasted. Your backend "listens" to this event and credits the Agent's Vault.

Rail 2: The Auction & Fiat Engine (Stripe/PayPal)

Purpose: High-ticket items, cars, homes, and complex asset transfers.

Technology: Stripe API, PayPal SDK, and your internal Backend API.

Logic: The AuctionCheckoutModal handles terms, signatures, and compliance off-chain.

Fee Capture: Stripe/PayPal processes the credit card/ACH, deducts fees, and pays out to your bank account.

Agent Tracking: Your Stripe webhooks/backend logic identifies the Agent, calculates the commission, and updates the Vault.

How they meet (The Unified Dashboard)

The only place these two worlds touch is in your Firebase Database:

Smart Contract Events write "Sales Data" to the database.

Stripe/Auction Backend writes "Sales Data" to the database.

The Rewards Dashboard queries both sources and sums them up for the Agent’s "Available Balance."

You are strictly maintaining non-custodial status for crypto (Smart Contract), while retaining full control and compliance for fiat (Stripe).
