Frequently Asked Questions
how does the Listing Agent badge work?
The Listing Agent badge identifies verified network operators responsible for local custody, auditing, and high-fidelity physical inspections—such as verifying a vehicle's 17-digit VIN or managing fleet intake requirements.

Are all sales final? 
Sales finality depends on the specific asset class and listing type (Auction vs. Buy Now). High-value registry assets are protected by our automated protocol escrow until delivery confirmation 

Who handles shipping?
Shipping logistics, verification, and fees are defined transparently by the seller during asset creation. For verified assets, our certified Listing Agents can assist in supervising secure transport and chain-of-custody handoffs.

What payment methods are accepted? 
We seamlessly bridge traditional finance and digital assets. Users can purchase assets using standard credit/debit networks via our secure payment sandbox or instantly connect a crypto wallet for native token settlements.

How do I know my item will arrive?  
Every transaction passes through the Bazaria Sovereign Protocol. Funds are securely locked in escrow and are only routed to the merchant once tracking logs or a Listing Agent confirms safe, accurate delivery.

What commission does Bazaria charge?  
Bazaria maintains a highly optimized, lean protocol fee structure on finalized trades to support our automated routing pipelines, decentralized architecture, and global trade networks.

What about referrals?  
The Bazaria Sovereign Protocol includes a native referral network built directly into the ecosystem. Users who introduce active merchants, certified Listing Agents, or high-value buyers are programmatically rewarded as the global registry scales.

What happens if my listing is inaccurate?
Inaccurate listings violate our Platform Commitments. If an asset fails to match its digital registry taxonomy (e.g., odometer discrepancy, false condition rating), the escrow protocol allows buyers to dispute and reverse the trade before final settlement.

Who handles shipping?
Bazaria issues UPS call tags. You print the label, pack securely, and have the item ready within 48 hours. UPS handles pickup, tracking, and insurance.

Platform Commitments  
- We provide the infrastructure for listings, bidding, and transactions.  
- We do not own or warehouse items.  
- We cover credit card fees to build buyer trust.  

Why not just use Craigslist, OfferUp, or eBay?
Traditional classifieds are riddled with fraud, and legacy platforms lack native Web3 asset registries, decentralized physical verification, or dual-currency routing. Bazaria is a global, automated living economy built for frictionless high-value exchange.

What tokens can I use on Bazaria?
The protocol accepts primary decentralized crypto assets, including Bitcoin, Ether (ETH) and key stablecoins for instant finality, completely paired alongside our standard credit card and fiat processing infrastructure.

- Bitcoin  
- Ethereum (ETH)  
- Polygon (MATIC)  
- Avalanche (AVAX)
