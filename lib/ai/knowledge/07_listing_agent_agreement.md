SECTION 2: AGE REQUISITE & METRIC-DRIVEN ACTIVITY LIFESPANS 

2.1 Minimum Age Constraint: To access corporate data loops and operate merchant intake modules,  all Listing Agents must be at least eighteen (18) years of age at the time of account creation. 2.2 The 2-Month Inactivity Auto-Purge: An Agent workspace remains active solely as long as  the Steward is actively engaged and producing marketplace volume. SECTION 3: THE PULSE RATING INTEGRITY & COMMISSION FORFEITURE RULES

 To maintain the elite reputation of our high-end verified marketplace, your financial payouts  are locked directly to your public Merchant Pulse Score ratings: 3.1 Authorized Payout Ratings: Bazaria distributes commission overrides and referral residuals  **only for completed transactions scoring POSITIVE or NEUTRAL customer pulse ratings.** 3.2 Negative Rating Penalty: Any transaction resulting in a verified NEGATIVE customer pulse rating  constitutes a performance failure. The system will automatically trigger a terminal override, terminate the account, and revoke all  platform access clearances immediately.

Agents can choose to configure their secure digital wallets to receive payouts using one of two  approved payment mechanisms: Dynamic Stablecoin Transfer (ERC-20 token routing directly to your wallet address)  or the physical Bazaria Sovereign Debit Card. 4.1 The $500 Debit Card Milestone Gate: To offset high-end secure card manufacturing costs, a Listing  Agent must clear an initial pipeline threshold of **Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00 USD)** in verified,  accumulated earnings before the protocol will authorize and issue a custom corporate debit card. 4.2 Separation Fund Exhaustion Rules: In the event that either party terminates this relationship,  the Agent retains the absolute right to continue using their issued physical debit card to clear  purchases until all existing, cleared vault funds are entirely exhausted. 4.3 Retained Micro-Balance Forfeiture: If an Agent chooses to separate or falls into permanent  inactivity *before* reaching the baseline $500.00 system threshold, no debit card will be issued,  and Bazaria will not credit, remit, or forward any remaining un-carded balances below that metric limit.  Balances under the threshold upon account termination are permanently forfeit to absorb setup overhead.
